Best Grind Size for Each Brewing Method

Brewing Method Grind Size Texture Brew Time
Espresso Fine (Very Fine) Like powder 25–30 sec
V60 / Drip Medium Like sand 2–3 min
French Press Coarse Like sea salt 4 min
Cold Brew Coarse Rough & chunky 8–12 hours
Moka Pot Medium-Fine Between fine & medium 1–2 min

☕ Did You Know?

“SAME COFFEE, DIFFERENT GRIND, COMPLETELY DIFFERENT TASTE.”

Grind size controls flavor
Wrong grind size can ruin good coffee
Coarser grind = lighter extraction
Each brewing method needs a specific grind size
Finer grind = stronger extraction
