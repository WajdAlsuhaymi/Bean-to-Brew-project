Arabica vs Robusta

Criteria Arabica Robusta
Acidity Higher – Bright & Fruity Lower – Mild
Sweetness More Natural Sweetness Less Sweet
Bitterness Mild Strong & Bold
Body Light to Medium Medium to Heavy
Aroma Complex & Aromatic Strong but Simpler
Aftertaste Smooth & Long-lasting Shorter & Strong

Coffee is not just one simple flavor. Its taste is shaped by different standards and bean types, each offering a unique experience. By understanding these characteristics, you can better appreciate the differences between Arabica and Robusta and begin to explore coffee with deeper knowledge.
