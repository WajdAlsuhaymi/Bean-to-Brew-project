Lesson 1

What is Coffee?

Discover where coffee begins and how this everyday drink starts its journey from plant to cup.

Introduction

Coffee is one of the most popular drinks in the world, enjoyed by millions every day. But coffee is more than just a drink. It begins as a seed inside a fruit called a coffee cherry, and goes through many stages before reaching your cup.

In this lesson, you will learn what coffee really is, where it comes from, and why understanding its origin is the first step to becoming more confident in the world of brewing.
