Popular Brewing Methods

Learn how different brewing styles change the same coffee beans into completely different cups — from light and smooth to rich and intense.

Popular Brewing Methods

Cold Brew

  • Coarse grind
  • Long extraction (8–12 hours)
  • Cold water brewing
  • Smooth and low acidity
  • Naturally sweet taste

French Press

  • Coarse grind
  • Immersion brewing method
  • 4-minute steeping time
  • Metal filter
  • Full body and rich texture

V60

  • Medium grind
  • Slow circular pouring
  • Paper filter used
  • Clean and balanced cup
  • Highlights flavor notes

Espresso

  • Fine grind
  • High pressure extraction
  • Short brew time (25–30 sec)
  • Strong and concentrated flavor
  • Thick crema layer

Hot Brew vs Cold Brew

Hot Brew ☕

  • Water temperature: 90–96°C
  • Extraction time: 2–5 minutes
  • Strong aroma and flavor
  • Used in V60, French Press, Espresso

Cold Brew ❄️

  • Cold or room temperature water
  • Extraction time: 8–12 hours
  • Smooth taste and low acidity
  • Perfect for refreshing iced coffee

Each brewing method creates a unique coffee experience. By understanding the differences in grind size, time, and technique, you can explore new flavors and find the method that best suits your taste.
